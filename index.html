ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Joshua "RED" Hughes! I completed my tattoo apprenticeship in 1999 from Chico, CA where I was born and raised. Then I moved to Sacramento were I worked in a couple different shops for numerous years. I then decided that I would like to travel the Country and see what else is out there. After traveling all of the great United States I decided to settle down in the little town of Lodi, CA. I also had numerous articles written about me, also has been a regular feature in local media and has had my art published nationally and internationally. I opened Emerald Tattoo in august 2008 and my great staff have built it into the empire it is today!
